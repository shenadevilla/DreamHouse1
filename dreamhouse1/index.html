DREAM HOUSE?

The target users of Dream House are the Pre-school children since they are the one who needs to learn counting, identifying letters and numbers. These children are mostly in the range of 3-5 years old.

But this game can also be played by all ages outside the mentioned age range with the guide of their parents as much as possible.

GOALS?

This game aims to help children learn how to count, and identify letters, shapes, and images.

This game helps children to identify how the letters, numbers, and words are pronounced.

This game allows the users to play and enjoy while learning something new. This also builds their excitement and curiosity in every progress they make.

thiS game let the children to learn from their mistakes and trying it to do it correctly the next time they try until they complete their dream house.
